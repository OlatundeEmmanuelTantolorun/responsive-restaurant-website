    History & Background

    Elitz Restaurant was founded by Olatunde Emmanuel with a vision to bring authentic African cuisine to the Surulere community.

    What began as a learning journey in web development evolved into a fully responsive restaurant website — built from scratch after months of dedication, trial, and growth.

    Founded in July 2025, Elitz represents more than food. It reflects passion, creativity, and the drive to build meaningful experiences through both programming and hospitality.
